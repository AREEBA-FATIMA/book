# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics** - where artificial intelligence meets the physical world.

## About This Book

This textbook provides a deep dive into the fascinating world of embodied artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics. From fundamental concepts to advanced applications, you'll gain a thorough understanding of how AI systems interact with and learn from the physical environment.

## What You'll Learn

This book is structured into **5 comprehensive chapters**:

### **Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI**
Explore the foundations of physical AI, embodied intelligence, and the intersection of AI with robotics.

### **Chapter 2: Mathematical Foundations**
Master the essential mathematics behind robotics - linear algebra, calculus, probability, and optimization techniques.

### **Chapter 3: Sensing and Perception**
Understand sensor technologies, computer vision, and how robots perceive their environment.

### **Chapter 4: Control and Actuation**
Learn control theory, motion planning, and actuation mechanisms that bring robots to life.

### **Chapter 5: Machine Learning for Robotics**
Discover how machine learning, reinforcement learning, and deep learning empower modern robotic systems.

## Getting Started

Navigate through the chapters using the sidebar on the left. Each chapter contains:

- **Theoretical foundations** with clear explanations
- **Mathematical formulations** for deeper understanding
- **Practical examples** and real-world applications
- **Exercises** to reinforce your learning

## Who Is This For?

This textbook is designed for:

- **Students** pursuing robotics, AI, or mechatronics
- **Researchers** exploring embodied AI systems
- **Engineers** developing autonomous robots
- **Enthusiasts** passionate about the future of robotics
